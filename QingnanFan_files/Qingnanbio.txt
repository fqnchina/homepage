Qingnan Fan is a PhD student in Shandong University advised by Prof. Baoquan Chen, working on computer vision and computational photography. He received his Bachelor degree in Shandong University, 2014. His research interests specifically lie in interactive real-time video segmentation, reflection removal, image smoothing, intrinsic image decomposition, and pratical application of his techniques on mobile devices. He received the Presidential Scholarship of Shandong University in 2015, which is awarded for the best students in Shandong University. He has research experiences in Tel Aviv University, Hebrew University of Jerusalem, Microsoft Research, Beijing Film Academy, etc.
